About Alexander International School

Alexander International School was founded with a vision to bridge quality education and moral integrity in Mbarara, Uganda. We offer a seamless educational journey from Nursery through Primary to Secondary (O & A Level). Our curriculum blends the National Curriculum of Uganda with international best practices.

We believe every child has unique potential. With small class sizes, well-trained teachers, and modern teaching aids, we foster critical thinking, creativity, and leadership.

Fully registered by Ministry of Education
Experienced faculty & low student-teacher ratio
